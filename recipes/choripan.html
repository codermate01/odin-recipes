Choripan

Description

The choripan is a very popular argentinian recipe, you should definitely try it, there's nothing better than eating a choripan after playing football or after work.

Is very simple to make it, just need a few ingredients and you're ready to taste one of the most flavorful dishes of Argentina.

Ingredients

  • Chorizo
  • Bread, I recommend to get like a bolillo bread or something similar

That's it, that fundamentally what you need for a choripan, although you can of course add more things like for example: chimichurri, ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise tomato, lettuce, garlic, onion, basically everything you want.
